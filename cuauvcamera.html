Design Process

Post-FDR Design Overview

Forward Facing Bracket-Camera Assembly

-Located in the UHPV and is top forward-facing camera that provides sterovision.

Forward-Facing Camera Enclosure

-Encloses the bottom forward-facing camera that provides sterovision with a tight waterproof seal.

Downward-Facing Camera Enclosure

-Encloses the downward-facing camera with a tight waterproof seal.

New Implemented Features

Vertical Plane for Sterovision

The forward-facing cameras are located in a colinear vertical plane that allows Aurora to produce depth maps from stereovision imaging.

Bulkhead Camera Cover

The bulkhead placed on the circular portion of the camera allows for the diameter of the enclosure to be reduced by more than 15% compared to previous designs.

Additional Wiring Space

The new downward facing camera design encorporates a larger endcap alleviating wiring problems by creating room for a 90° ethernet cable connector adatper and allowing for a larger bend radius on the SEACON cables.

Color Palette Air-Water Color Conversion

The Color Palette is a newly added attachment to CUAUV submarines and uses known stickers with known value colors to create an underwater color correction (removing the blue tint).

Epoxy Mold for Color Palette

The Mold for the Color Palette is a 3D printed part that is used to form the general shape of the Color Palette by using a clear epoxy mixture.

Future Improvements

O-Ring Seals

As a result of the new downward facing camera enclosure design, it increases the probability for a leakage as a result of an additional bore seal for the endcap. If a design in the future could implement a spacious design for wires that only uses one seal that would be amazing.

Brackets

In the past, all brackets were machined out of aluminum; however, this year I changed the design to be 3D printable. As a result, it saves a lot of machining time, weight, and could be easily reproduced. One improvement though would be to use Generative Design and Topology Optimization to optimally design the bracket.
